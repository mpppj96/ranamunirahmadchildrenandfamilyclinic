Common Inquiries

What are Dr. Munir Ahmad Rana's qualifications?
Dr. Rana holds M.B.B.S. from King Edward Medical University, Lahore (1999). He is a Child Specialist, Family Physician, and currently Additional Medical Superintendent & Director Emergency at Govt. Sir Ganga Ram Hospital.
What are the clinic timings?
Monday to Saturday: 7:00 PM – 11:00 PM. Emergency consultations can be arranged via phone on Sundays.
Is the clinic equipped for pediatric emergencies?
Yes, basic emergency equipment, nebulizers, and vaccination cold chain are available. For major emergencies, Dr. Rana coordinates with Sir Ganga Ram Hospital.
Where exactly is the clinic located?
The clinic is located at Cantt View Phase 1, 2 Street, Cantt View Housing Scheme, Lahore. Use the map above for precise directions or open Google Maps with the button provided.
Do I need an appointment?
Appointments are recommended to reduce waiting time, but walk-ins are welcome. Evening slots are busy, so booking via call or WhatsApp is ideal.
